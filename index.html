About Me

I have always tried leaning toward the side of optimism with everything life throw's my way. Yes, sometimes challenges make that hard, but I truly believe things happen for a reason. can't explain it, but it's true.

I live by having the philosophy of never taking anything for granted. I want to pay my knowledge forward (all things technical) to anyone who'd like to learn.

When I take on a project for someone, whether it be to design a website, build a form, or something else, I try to ensure that the finished project is exactly what the customer wanted providing functionality, usability, and being pleasing to the eye. When building a site, my goal is to build a site where anyone from any level of expertise will find things fast and easily. I am an extremely organized person and I work hard at trying to make life easier for my clients and their target market.

I have always believed that 'Everything you do has a consequence on the future' so make sure you try your hardest the first time to do the best job ossible because you may not have another chance. I try to do that for all my customers.
